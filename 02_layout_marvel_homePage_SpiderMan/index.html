SPIDERMAN

Spider-Man, a superhero character created by Stan Lee for comic books published by Marvel Comics, has appeared in film since CBS's 1977 television film pilot, for program The Amazing Spider-Man. After protracted litigation over the Spider-Man copyrights, Sony Pictures and its subsidiary Columbia obtained the film rights for $7 million from Marvel in 1999
